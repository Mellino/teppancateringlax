Information about Events

  • We have a minimum requirement of 15 people per event.
  • When the number of people exceeds 30, another chef is required (No Additional Cost).
  • The remaining balance must be paid in cash on the day of the event.
PLEASE NOTE : WE DO NOT PROVIDE TABLES OR CHAIRS FOR YOUR GUESTS

Additional Fees

  • A $100 travel fee will be automatically added for locations more than 25 miles from Monrovia CA.
  • A $100 late fee will be charged for guests arriving over an hour late.
